IGRA status and microbiome composition

Adonis test

There are seemingly innumerable ways to query for differences in microbial community composition. Given the zero-inflated type of microbiome data, non-parametric analyses are most appropriate to ask if a statistical difference exists between two groups. In the case of IGRA status, we concluded that LTBI (being IGRA+) has no detectable effect on intestinal microbiome composition. Here are some of the ways we can use to reproduce that analysis.
